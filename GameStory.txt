You wake up in this new world (Make a world Name)
You are welcomed by a godess. This godess is the one you choose to pray to at the begining.

At the center of this world there is a tower with X amount of floors.
Each floor has various Monster.
The high the floor number the more elite the monsters.

You enter the club of the godess that awakens you bringing her "Back to life"
Bringing her back to Life
Clubs which Pray to their own godesses Giving them life.

The story has you progress through the game increasing yourself in power and exploring various possibilities with you "god club"
The ultimate goal is to finish all x number of floor in the tower.
Once you finish the floor you are given a key and are let into the great Palace where the "game maker" lives.


Once you finish the 80 floors the doors of the great palace open to you.

You can go in whenever you find yourself ready.
And as you go through the rooms in the palace you find out the true story behind the world you are in.
